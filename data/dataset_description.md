## Description
The dataset represents a **survey responses about personal financial behavior and investment preferences**, not a financial transaction data.
It includes demographic information and responses to questions about investment goals, risk perception, and financial decision-making.

Information collected from anonymous respondents includes:
- Gender and age
- Interest in investment opportunities
- Ranking of investment instruments
- Investment goals and expectations
- Reasons for choosing specific investment options
- Monitoring and management of investments
- Sources of financial information

## Purpose in This Project

The dataset is used to understand how demographic factors, especially gender and age, influence investment preferences, financial goals, and decision-making behavior.

- Note: The dataset contains a relatively small sample size (40 responses), 
therefore results should be interpreted as exploratory rather than statistically representative.
